Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Classic Spaghetti Carbonara: A rich and creamy pasta dish made with spaghetti, crispy pancetta, and a sauce of eggs, pecorino, and parmesan cheese. It's finished with a sprinkle of black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese, grated
  • 50g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta or guanciale until crispy, then add the crushed garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the grated pecorino and parmesan cheeses. Season with black pepper.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix with the pancetta in the pan. Remove from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture until creamy.
  5. Serve immediately with extra grated cheese on top.
