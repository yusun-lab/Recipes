Chicken Alfredo

Description

A comforting dish of fettuccine pasta tossed in a creamy Alfredo sauce made from heavy cream, parmesan cheese, and butter, topped with sliced cooked chicken and garnished with parsley.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 300g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then cook in a pan until fully cooked. Slice into strips.
  3. In the same pan, melt the butter and cook the minced garlic until fragrant. Add the heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in the grated parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth. Add the cooked fettuccine and chicken strips to the sauce.
  5. Mix well and serve hot with chopped parsley on top.
